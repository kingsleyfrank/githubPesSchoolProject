About Our Restaurant

We offer the best Italian dishes in a friendly and calm atmosphere.

La Pesto is located at The Janitero Hotel, and is a delightful restaurant with comfortable and stylish furniture. The decor is artistic, and with great ambiance. Its own “la fresco terrace”, is an ideal place for a great daytime business lunch or just a casual affair with friends!.

The Italian menu is thoughtfully tailored to tempt, with an eye for wholesome organic options which are healthy, tasty and beautifully presented. Enjoy the opportunity to please your taste buds La Pesto classic pizza cooked in an Italian wood burning oven. The La Pesto restaurant also offers a wide variety of wines.
